Study tips that work

  • Test yourself — retrieval beats re-reading. Quiz first, then study what you miss.
  • Space it out — short, repeated sessions stick better than one long cram.
  • Review your misses — the Review Missed mode resurfaces weak spots until you master them.
  • Read the why — every answer explains the ruling, not just the result.
